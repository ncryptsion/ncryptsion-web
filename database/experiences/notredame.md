I voluntarily contributed to the development of a custom Discord bot for my school's Junior High School Discord Server. The bot includes a variety of commands for ID verification, student management and server moderation. Designed with a focus on privacy and efficiency, it enhances the overall user experience by easing essential tasks such as moderation and utilities, helping to maintain an organized and secure environment.

# 📋 Additional Responsibilities
- Ensuring the privacy and safety of the users.
- Ensuring that only students from the school is allowed.
- Management of events and activities.
- Server growth suggestions and implementation.
